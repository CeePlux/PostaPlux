General Questions

Is PostaPlux completely free to use?


Yes! PostaPlux is 100% free and will always remain free. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.


Do I need to create an account?

No account required! PostaPlux works entirely in your browser. You can start creating screenshots immediately without any registration or login.


What browsers are supported?


PostaPlux works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For best performance, we recommend using the latest version of your browser.

Features & Usage


How do I download my screenshot?


After generating your screenshot, click the "Download PNG" button. The image will be saved to your device with a filename like "facebook-post-[timestamp].png".


Can I use my own profile picture?


Yes! Click the "Upload Photo" button in the Profile Picture section to upload any image from your device. You can also remove it anytime with the "Remove" button.


How do I preserve line breaks in my text?


Simply press Enter/Return while typing in the text area. PostPlux automatically preserves all line breaks in both the preview and downloaded screenshot.


What resolution are the screenshots?


Screenshots are generated at high resolution (2x scale) ensuring crisp, clear images suitable for both web and print use.


Privacy & Security


Is my data safe with PostaPlux?


Absolutely! All screenshot generation happens locally in your browser. We don't store your images, text, or any personal data on our servers.


What happens to my uploaded profile picture?


Uploaded images are only stored temporarily in your browser's memory and are not uploaded to any server. They disappear when you close the page or remove them.


Can I use the screenshots for projects, social media, or school work?


Yes! You are free to use the generated screenshots for personal, educational, or professional use.


Technical Issues


Screenshot generation isn't working. What should I do?


Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Make sure you have internet connection (for loading libraries)
If problems persist, contact us with details.


Why does the watermark appear on screenshots?


The "PostaPlux" watermark is a subtle credit to our tool. It appears with reduced opacity at the top-right corner and doesn't interfere with your content.


Can I use PostPlux on mobile?


Yes! PostaPlux was actually developed on a mobile phone and is fully responsive. It works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.
